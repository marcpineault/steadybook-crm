The Journey

How a Lead Becomes a Client

From first touch to signed deal — every step is AI-assisted with human approval gates.

🌐

Lead Arrives

Website form, quiz, referral email, or Outlook booking. Automatically captured, scored, and entered into the pipeline.

🧠

AI Analyzes & Scores

GPT extracts facts into the Memory Engine. Lead scoring ranks by deal size, urgency, stage probability, and historical win rates.

✉️

Auto-Draft Follow-Up

AI generates a personalized follow-up email using client memory, conversation history, and learning from past performance.

👆

Marc Approves via Telegram

One tap: Approve, Skip, or Snooze. Every action feeds the learning loop. Approved emails auto-send via Resend for website leads.

📊

Engagement Tracked

Email opens, clicks, bounces tracked via Resend webhooks. Response outcomes logged. The system learns what works.

📋

Meeting Prep Auto-Generated

1 hour before any meeting, Marc gets a prep doc: client snapshot, interaction history, recommended agenda, objection prep, personal touch.

🎉

Deal Closed → Referral Loop

Win/loss logged. At 2-4 weeks and 3-4 months post-close, the morning briefing surfaces referral opportunities automatically.
